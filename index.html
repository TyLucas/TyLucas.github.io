Mac and Cheese

  • Time: 15:00
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Elbow Noodle 2 cups
Cheddar Cheese 1 cup
Milk 1 cups

Step 1: Boil water.
Step 2: Pour noodles in water.
Step 3: Heat up milk and cheese in another pot.
Step 4: Mix them together.

Buffalo Wings

  • Time: 30:00
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken wings 2 pounds
Red Hot Half a cup
Butter 1 stick
Flour 4 cups
Seasonings Personal Preference
Frying Oil 1 pot full

Step 1: Melt butter and mix in Red Hot.
Step 2: Dredge wings in flour mixture.
Step 3: Fry wings in oil
Step 4: Toss wings in sauce

Source: Buffalo Wing Recipe
